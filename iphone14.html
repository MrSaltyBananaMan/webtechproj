The iPhone 14 is an incremental upgrade. The iPhone 14 adds one additional core to the GPU processor, utilizes e-SIM technology, sports slightly better battery life, and has Apple's new safety features like satellite connectivity for emergency communications and crash detection.

However, the base iPhone 14 itself isn't much different from the iPhone 13 from last year. It uses the same A15 Bionic chip as the iPhone 13, and its primary and ultrawide cameras still max out at 12 megapixels. Also like the iPhone 13, the iPhone 14 is available in 128 gigabyte, 256 GB, and 512 GB sizes.
